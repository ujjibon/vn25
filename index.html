About Us

Our Mission: A Call to Action

The 4th National Earth Fest is more than an event; it's a movement. Our mission is to unite the youth of the nation, empowering them to be the vanguard of change. Through creativity, innovation, and knowledge, we aim to ignite a nationwide passion for sustainability and drive tangible action against plastic pollution.

About Viqarunnisa Noon Earth Club (VNEC)

Established with the vision of fostering environmental consciousness, VNEC has been at the forefront of youth-led green initiatives. We believe in the power of education and action to create lasting change. This festival is our flagship event, designed to provide a national platform for students to innovate, to speak, and to lead the charge for a cleaner planet.
